Polling Place

Model-Generated Definition:

Equivalent to Polling Location.

A kind of Building in Role. A valid occurrence satisfies the following necessary conditions:

  • staffed by at least one occurrence of Poll Worker.
  • serves at least one occurrence of Voter.
  • serves precinct at least one occurrence of Precinct.

A valid occurrence may also have the following properties:

  • any number of occurrences of In Person Voting.
  • has election any number of occurrences of Election.

Definition: Location at which voters cast ballots in-person on vote-capture devices (e.g., DRE) under the supervision of poll workers usually on election day. Syn: polling station or poll. Note: A polling place is sometimes in 1-to-1 correspondence with a precinct but in some cases may represent multiple precincts as with vote centers.

Definition Origin: NIST SP 1500-100
